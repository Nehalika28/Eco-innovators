The Problem

India's Unmanaged Waste Crisis

Overflowing Landfills

Over 90% of waste is dumped unsegregated, leading to massive, toxic landfills that pollute air, water, and soil.

Contaminated Recyclables

When wet waste mixes with dry waste, it contaminates valuable recyclables like paper and plastic, making them unusable.

Inefficient Collection

Municipal trucks follow fixed routes, often visiting half-empty bins or missing overflowing ones, wasting fuel and time.

Our Solution

An Integrated Smart Ecosystem

SwachhMitra tackles the problem at every stage, from disposal to collection. We combine hardware, AI, and a user-centric app to create a closed-loop system for waste management.

Automatic Segregation

Onboard AI identifies waste (wet, dry, plastic) and sorts it automatically.

Public Gamification

A mobile app rewards users with "Green Points" for correct disposal.

Dynamic Route Optimization

IoT sensors report fill-levels for AI-powered, efficient collection routes.

SwachhMitra System Flow

1
User scans QR & disposes waste.
2
AI Camera identifies & sorts waste (Wet, Dry, Plastic).
3
User receives "Green Points" on the app.
4
IoT sensor reports bin fill-level to cloud dashboard.
5
AI generates optimized route for collection truck.

Core Features

Technology in Action

AI Waste Identification

A high-speed camera paired with a lightweight Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) instantly identifies the waste type as it falls.

Accuracy: 95%+

Motorized Sorting Flaps

Based on the AI's signal, high-torque motorized flaps direct the waste into the correct compartment (Wet, Dry, or Plastic) in milliseconds.

Sorting Time: < 1 sec

Real-Time IoT Sensors

Ultrasonic sensors in each compartment measure fill levels. This data is transmitted in real-time to a central dashboard via a cellular (4G/5G) module.

Connectivity: 99.9% Uptime

Driving Participation

Gamification for a Greener City

To create lasting behavioral change, we need to make segregation rewarding. Our app turns recycling into a game.

Scan & Earn

Users scan their unique QR code on the app before disposing waste to identify themselves.

Earn "Green Points"

For every correct disposal (e.g., wet waste in the smart bin), users are awarded "Green Points."

Redeem Rewards

Points can be redeemed for discounts and offers at local businesses, creating a circular community economy.

For Municipalities

AI-Powered Route Optimization

Our central dashboard gives municipal authorities a real-time "digital twin" of the city's waste infrastructure. AI analyzes fill-level data from all smart bins to generate the most fuel-efficient collection routes, dispatching trucks only where they are needed.
